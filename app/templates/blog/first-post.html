Understanding Fair Division

Posted on March 19, 2024

What is Fair Division?

Fair division is the problem of dividing a set of items among a group of people in a way that is fair to all participants. This problem arises in many real-world scenarios, from dividing an inheritance to splitting household chores.

Types of Items

In fair division, we deal with two types of items:

  • Divisible items can be split into fractions (like a cake or money)
  • Indivisible items must be given whole to one person (like a car or house)

How Our Calculator Works

Our fair division calculator uses mathematical optimization to find the fairest possible division of items. It takes into account:

  • Each person's valuation of each item
  • Whether items are divisible or indivisible
  • The goal of maximizing the minimum satisfaction

Example

Let's say we have three people and three items:

  • Alice, Bob, and Clare
  • A cake (divisible)
  • A car (indivisible)
  • A house (indivisible)

Each person assigns a value to each item, and our calculator finds the fairest way to divide everything.
